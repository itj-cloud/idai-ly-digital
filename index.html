Common Questions

What do I need to provide?

expand_more

Just the basics — your business name, logo if you have one, a few photos (or I can use stock), your services, phone number, and what you want customers to do when they land on your site. I handle everything else.

Do you offer support after launch?

expand_more

Yes. The $55/month maintenance plan covers hosting, content updates, and basic upkeep so your site stays current without you having to deal with any of the tech.

Can I update my site myself?

expand_more

Yes — if you're on the maintenance plan, just send over what you need changed and it gets handled for you. Prefer to manage it yourself? iDai.ly Digital will hand over the full website code and all rights so you can host and manage it anywhere you'd like. You own it completely either way.

Is maintenance mandatory?

expand_more

Not at all. Maintenance is recommended because it keeps your site running smoothly without any effort on your end. It includes hosting, content updates, and basic upkeep for $55/month. If you'd prefer to manage things yourself, we hand over the full code and all rights — no strings attached.

Do you work with businesses outside my area?

expand_more

Yes — everything is handled remotely so location is never an issue. I work with small businesses nationwide.
